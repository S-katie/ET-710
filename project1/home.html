History of Nassau

Nassau has a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1670. Originally a settlement for the British, Nassau became a hub for pirates during the 18th century. Its colonial past is reflected in the architecture, such as the iconic Government House and Fort Fincastle. Over time, Nassau evolved into a bustling port city and eventually the capital of The Bahamas.

Top Attractions in Nassau

1. Nassau Beaches

Enjoy Nassau's stunning beaches, such as Cable Beach and Paradise Island Beach. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

2. The Atlantis Resort

A world-famous resort offering luxury accommodations, water parks, aquariums, and an array of entertainment options.

3. Pirates Museum

Explore the fascinating history of piracy in Nassau, with interactive exhibits and historical artifacts.

4. Nassau Straw Market

Shop for handcrafted Bahamian goods at this vibrant market. A great place to find unique souvenirs!
