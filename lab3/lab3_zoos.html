Zoos in New York City

The Bronx Zoo

The Bronx Zoo is one of the largest zoos in the world, featuring over 6,000 animals from around the globe. It offers a variety of exhibits, including the African Plains, Tiger Mountain, and JungleWorld.

Visit the Bronx Zoo

Central Park Zoo

The Central Park Zoo is located within Central Park and is home to a variety of species, including snow leopards, penguins, and sea lions. It's a perfect spot for families visiting the park.

Visit the Central Park Zoo

Queens Zoo

Located in Flushing Meadows-Corona Park, the Queens Zoo features animals from North and South America, including bison, pumas, and Andean condors. It also has a farm area where children can interact with domestic animals.

Visit the Queens Zoo

Staten Island Zoo

The Staten Island Zoo is known for its collection of reptiles and hands-on exhibits for children. It's a small but charming zoo with a focus on educating visitors about wildlife conservation.

Visit the Staten Island Zoo
